Satulan Ayurvedic Clinics

Ayurveda, the science of a holistic life, has been practiced by doctors in India for thousands of years. Our team of highly qualified doctors follow this medical system and have gained experience under Shree Guru Dr. Balaji Tambe. At Santulan Ayurveda Clinics we specialize in the treatment of chronic diseases and place great emphasis on disease prevention and the imparting of the knowledge of self-healing.
